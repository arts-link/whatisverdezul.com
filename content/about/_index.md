Verdèzul is a Los Angeles based duo comprised of two artists, Jay Stevens and WI$E. They hail from Carson & Inglewood, respectively. Their goal is to not only take the entertainment industry by storm, but to change the overall landscape of music. Their first official single, "Taxes Finnah Hit" released in 2023, spearheaded their musical journey. Shortly afterward, they continued to flex their musical prowess and creativity with the release of the Green Pack EP. We are in the world, not of the world, but we reflect the world. The Earth is Green and Blue. This is Verdèzul.
