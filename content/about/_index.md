Verdèzul is a Los Angeles based duo comprised of two artists, Jay Stevens and WI$E. With both hailing from Carson & Inglewood respectively, their goal is to not only take the entertainment industry by storm, but change the overall landscape of music. Their first official single "Taxes Finnah Hit" released in 2023, which spearheaded their musical journey. They continued to flex their musical prowess and creativity with their release of the Green Pack EP shortly afterward. They make music in the world, though not of the world, yet is still for the world. The Earth is Green and Blue. This is Verdèzul.
